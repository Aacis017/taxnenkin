Pension Lumpsum

Our service assists individuals leaving Japan in claiming their Shakai Hoken (Nenkin Lump-Sum Withdrawal) or retirement contributions refund. Upon departure, whether temporarily or permanently, you can initiate the process to claim your tax refund, typically after residing outside Japan for 6-8 months. You have a two-year window from your departure date to apply for your pension refund, ensuring you can manage your retirement funds efficiently and effectively. Let us simplify the process and ensure you receive the benefits you're entitled to promptly and securely.
